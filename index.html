Overview

In "Lika and Purri's Great Rescue," an unexpected accident sets the stage for an exhilarating adventure. The game begins with a dramatic house fire at Leakey's residence, where Purri, our playful hero, finds herself drenched while exploring the mysterious spaces within the walls.

Game Features

  • Engaging Gameplay: Help Purri escape from tricky situations and solve intriguing mysteries hidden within the walls.
  • Beautiful Graphics: Enjoy stunning visuals that bring the adventure to life.
  • Interactive Storyline: Experience a captivating story filled with surprises and twists.
